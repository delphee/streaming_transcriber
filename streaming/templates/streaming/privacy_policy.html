Privacy Policy

Appleton Campbell Field Service Management Portal

Last Updated: November 2025
Effective Date: November 2025
Internal Use Only: This privacy policy applies to Appleton Campbell employees using the internal Field Service Management Portal. This is a business tool for authorized personnel only.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Appleton Campbell ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information through our internal Field Service Management Portal (the "System"). This System is designed exclusively for use by Appleton Campbell employees in the course of their work duties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Employee Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, employee ID, ServiceTitan technician ID
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, authentication tokens, device tokens for push notifications
  • Activity Data: Login times, feature usage, system interactions

2.2 Job Site Data

  • Audio Recordings: Conversations recorded during customer interactions at job sites
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of recorded conversations, generated automatically
  • Job Information: Appointment details, job IDs, dispatch information synchronized from ServiceTitan
  • AI Analysis: Automated summaries, action items, key topics, and sentiment analysis of conversations

2.3 Customer Information (from ServiceTitan)

  • Customer Data: Names, addresses, phone numbers, and service history
  • Location Data: Service addresses and location information
  • Financial Data: Invoice information, estimates, and payment history

2.4 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, mobile device tokens
  • Usage Data: Access logs, error reports, performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Business Operations

  • Facilitate field service operations and job tracking
  • Generate transcriptions and analyses of customer interactions
  • Provide technicians with customer history and job context
  • Track appointment status and dispatch workflows
  • Send push notifications for job status updates

3.2 Quality Improvement

  • Analyze conversations for training and quality assurance
  • Identify coaching opportunities for technicians
  • Improve customer service delivery
  • Enhance AI analysis and transcription accuracy

3.3 System Administration

  • Maintain and improve system functionality
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Ensure system security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Monitor system performance and usage

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Heroku (database and application hosting)
  • File Storage: Amazon S3 (audio recordings and documents)
  • AI Processing: OpenAI and AssemblyAI for transcription and analysis

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access limited to authorized personnel only
  • Private S3 storage with presigned URLs

4.3 Data Retention

  • Audio Chunks: Automatically deleted after 7 days (transcripts retained)
  • Conversations: Retained for 30 days unless marked for permanent storage
  • Permanently Saved Items: Retained indefinitely until manually deleted
  • Job Documents: ServiceTitan data retained for business purposes
  • Old Dispatch Jobs: Automatically cleaned up after 7 days

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Internal Sharing

Information may be shared within Appleton Campbell with:

  • Supervisors and managers for quality assurance and coaching
  • Administrative staff for operational purposes
  • IT personnel for system maintenance and support

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • ServiceTitan: CRM and job management integration
  • OpenAI: AI-powered conversation analysis
  • AssemblyAI: Audio transcription services
  • Amazon Web Services: Secure file storage
  • Heroku: Application hosting and database
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Mobile notifications

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 As an Employee

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Questions: You can contact HR or IT with privacy concerns

6.2 Limitations

As this is a business tool required for job functions, some data collection is mandatory for employment purposes. However, we collect only information necessary for legitimate business purposes.

7. Customer Privacy

Employees must handle customer information responsibly:

  • Only access customer data necessary for your job duties
  • Do not share customer information outside of approved business channels
  • Inform customers when conversations are being recorded
  • Follow all Appleton Campbell policies regarding customer data protection

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the System constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Contact your supervisor, HR department, or IT department
